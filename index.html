01 — About

Where data
meets impact

My work sits at the intersection of quantitative analysis and social impact — ESG scoring at a Berlin-based VC, political risk modelling across 51 emerging markets, pro-bono strategy consulting for financial abuse survivors, and assistive technology research that earned a Springer publication with 1500+ downloads. I've also spent years in the field — 33 countries, 500+ people supported directly, and enough impact reports to know that the hardest part of data work isn't the model. It's making the answer matter to someone.

Following a period of significant personal challenge, I stepped back from employment to focus on recovery, independent learning, and fieldwork. During this time I completed data science upskilling across ML, deep learning, and generative AI; travelled extensively across 33 countries building cross-cultural perspective on development and inequality; and volunteered consistently with GoodGym, completing 50+ missions supporting underserved communities in London. All 6 projects in this portfolio were built during this period.

Whether the output is a Python model, a strategy deck, or a field report that moves people — the question underneath is always the same: What does this data mean for the people it's supposed to serve?

Deep Learning
Equity Price Forecasting

Production-deployed deep learning research dashboard benchmarking 7 neural architectures — LSTM, Stacked LSTM, SimpleRNN & 1-D CNN — against 45 years of AAPL OHLCV data. Features 3-stage hyperparameter optimisation (GridSearchCV × Keras Tuner Hyperband), multi-step 2-day forecasting heads, 95% heteroskedastic prediction intervals, rolling realised volatility, and chronological walk-forward backtesting. Live-deployed on Streamlit Cloud · auto-deploy from GitHub.

ESG Scoring Dashboard

ESG scoring and analytics platform for 20 globally listed companies across 15 ESG signals. NLP & Graph Intelligence
Misinformation Spread Tracker

End-to-end misinformation pipeline combining graph-theoretic diffusion modelling (PageRank, Louvain community detection), TF-IDF + heuristic NLP credibility scoring, Random Forest ensemble classification (0.91+ CV AUC-ROC), and SHAP game-theoretic explainability. Computationally replicates Vosoughi et al. (Science, 2018) — fake cascades propagate 3× deeper and 6× broader than real news.

Emerging Markets
Emerging Markets Investment Visualiser

Full stack ML investment intelligence tool across 51 emerging economies, engineering 12 macroeconomic and governance features from World Bank WDI, IMF WEO, and WB WGI datasets. KMeans clustering (k=4), L2-regularised Ridge Regression (CV R² = 0.9996), and PCA dimensionality reduction segment markets into investment tiers and surface feature importance. Visualised in an 11-tab React dashboard — including a custom SVG Opportunity Matrix, multi-axis Risk Dashboard, correlation heatmap, and PCA scatter — deployed via GitHub CI/CD to Vercel with a Vite build pipeline.

That's impact measurement in its most direct form.
